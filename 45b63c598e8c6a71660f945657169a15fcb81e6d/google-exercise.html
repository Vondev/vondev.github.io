Maintenance Request App

Prompt — A great residential community (e.g. condominium, college dormitory, etc.) provides a variety of amenities and a dedicated staff responsible for its maintenance. But there isn’t always an easy way for community members to report problems to management so they can be resolved quickly.

Overview

The community I chose for this exercise is 6TenEast, a residential apartment complex located in Sunnyvale, CA. I chose this prompt and community because I the greatest access to its users and potential stakeholders. In all, this exercise took approximately 20 hours.

Finding The Problem

Located in the heard of Silicon Valley, 6TenEast is considered a luxury apartment complex. It's rental offerings feature semi-furnished apartments that come with most electronic appliances installed.

As I began my research, I found out that the complex already has an existing web portal that on-site maintenance team in charge of . My job is to figure out how to improve this existing process to make it a better

6TenEast is considered a luxury apartment complex and has a existing management team in charge of. To begin, I interviewed both the condo management and 3 residents. While the exercise is geared towards designing for residents, I found it useful to interview the property manager because this problem the workflow of the management plays a huge role in the resident's experience .
