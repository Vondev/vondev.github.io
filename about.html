My name is Jesse and I am a designer currently based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I love to process the needs of users, stakeholders, and businesses into usable, effective, and visually appealing solutions. My previous companies and clients include Assurant Digital, Microsoft Research, Lowe's, and Halliburton.

With a background in both Human-Computer Interaction and Software Engineering, I enjoy every aspect of the design process from research to design to implementation. I especially delight in the dual challenge of crafting polished interfaces that look and feel good to the user while furthering the needs of the business.

I am currently on the lookout for opportunities in UX, UI, and Product Design. Feel free to reach out to me anytime!
